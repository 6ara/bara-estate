Overview

Bara Estate is a family-owned farm located in the heart of the Rift Valley Nandi County. We specialize in organic fruits and vegetables, grass-fed beef,free-range poultryand a litte bit of dairy farming. Our mission is to provide our customers with high-quality, locally-sourced products while promoting sustainable farming practices.
